# Privacy Policy — SnapStage

This privacy policy applies to the SnapStage app ("Application") for mobile devices, created by Asiye ("Service Provider") as a freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

## Summary

SnapStage is an AI-powered virtual staging tool for real-estate professionals. To function, it sends the room photos you choose to stage to a third-party AI service provider (Google) for processing, and returns AI-generated, virtually staged versions of those photos. This policy explains what is collected, how, why, and with whom it is shared.

## Information the Application Collects, and How

**1. Staging data** — collected only when you actively use the staging feature:

- Room photos you capture with the camera or select from your photo library for staging
- Listing details you enter (listing name, notes, room types)
- The AI-generated staged images produced from your photos

**2. Automatically collected data:**

- Device information (model, operating system version, language) and IP address
- Usage data (screens visited, features used, session duration, time and date)
- Crash and diagnostic data
- Advertising-related identifiers, only if you grant App Tracking Transparency permission

**3. Purchase data:** subscription status and transaction identifiers. Card details never reach the Service Provider.

## How the Information Is Used

- To generate virtually staged images: your room photos are processed by AI services solely for this purpose
- To operate, maintain, and improve the Application (analytics, crash diagnostics)
- To manage subscriptions and the free-usage entitlement
- To measure advertising effectiveness (only with your App Tracking Transparency consent)
- The Service Provider may use contact information you provide to send important notices

## Third Parties That Process Data

The Application shares data with the following processors, strictly to provide the service. Each provider is bound by data-protection terms that provide protection equal to or greater than this policy:

- [Google LLC](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) — Gemini AI models via Firebase AI Logic / Google Cloud process the room photos you submit to generate staged versions. Google also provides Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config, App Check, Anonymous Authentication, and Cloud Messaging for app operation.
- [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) — subscription management
- Apple — payment processing through the App Store
- [Meta Platforms](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy) — advertising measurement, only with App Tracking Transparency consent

The Service Provider may also disclose information as required by law (for example, to comply with a subpoena), to protect rights or safety, to investigate fraud, or to respond to a government request.

## Property Photos

Photos you submit may depict the interiors of private property. They are processed solely to provide the service, are never sold, and are not used for advertising. Only upload photos you own or have the right to use, and avoid photos that contain identifiable people, documents, or other personal information. You are responsible for having the necessary authority to photograph and process images of the properties you work with.

## AI-Generated Images and Disclosure

Every image the Application generates is virtually staged by AI and carries an embedded "Virtually Staged" label. Staged images do not reflect the real, physical condition of a property. When you publish them, you are responsible for disclosing that they are virtually staged, as required by applicable rules (for example NAR ethics rules and MLS policies).

## Consent

Before the Application can be used, an in-app notice describes this processing — including the AI processing of your photos — and requires your explicit agreement. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your data in the Application and discontinuing use of the Application.

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Room photos sent for staging are not retained by the Service Provider after processing
- Your listings, original photos, and staged images are stored only locally on your device; delete individual listings or images at any time in the Application
- Automatically collected data is retained per the third-party providers' policies
- To request deletion of any data, contact the Service Provider at asiygy@icloud.com

## Security

The Service Provider provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the information it processes and maintains. Data in transit is encrypted.

## Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by uninstalling the Application.

The Application is not directed at children under 13, and the Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
