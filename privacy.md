# Privacy Policy — DocMind

This privacy policy applies to the DocMind app ("Application") for mobile devices, created by Asiye ("Service Provider") as a freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

## Summary

DocMind is a document intelligence tool: you import documents, and the Application uses AI to extract their content, answer your questions with source citations, and generate structured insights (summaries, key points, actions, flashcards, quizzes). To function, it sends the content of the documents you import and your questions about them to a third-party AI service provider (Google) for processing. This policy explains what is collected, how, why, and with whom it is shared.

## Information the Application Collects, and How

**1. Document and chat data** — collected only when you actively import a document or use the chat/insights features:

- The content of documents you choose to import (extracted text and page images from PDFs, photos, and other files)
- Your questions and chat messages about those documents
- The structured outputs generated from them (summaries, key points, actions, flashcards, quizzes)

**2. Automatically collected data:**

- Device information (model, operating system version, language) and IP address
- Usage data (screens visited, features used, session duration, time and date)
- Crash and diagnostic data
- Advertising-related identifiers, only if you grant App Tracking Transparency permission

**3. Purchase data:** subscription status and transaction identifiers. Card details never reach the Service Provider.

## How the Information Is Used

- To generate answers and insights: document content and your questions are processed by AI services solely for this purpose
- To operate, maintain, and improve the Application (analytics, crash diagnostics)
- To manage subscriptions and the free-trial entitlement
- To measure advertising effectiveness (only with your App Tracking Transparency consent)
- The Service Provider may use contact information you provide to send important notices

## Third Parties That Process Data

The Application shares data with the following processors, strictly to provide the service. Each provider is bound by data-protection terms that provide protection equal to or greater than this policy:

- [Google LLC](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) — Gemini AI models via Firebase AI Logic / Google Cloud process the content of imported documents and your questions to generate answers and insights. Google also provides Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config, and Cloud Messaging for app operation.
- [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) — subscription management
- Apple — payment processing through the App Store
- [Meta Platforms](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy) — advertising measurement, only with App Tracking Transparency consent

The Service Provider may also disclose information as required by law (for example, to comply with a subpoena), to protect rights or safety, to investigate fraud, or to respond to a government request.

## Sensitive Information in Documents

Documents you import may contain personal or sensitive information. Their content is processed solely to provide the service, is never sold, and is not used for advertising. Do not import documents containing other people's sensitive personal data (identity numbers, health or financial records) unless you have the necessary authority and legal basis to process them.

## Consent

Before the Application can be used, an in-app consent notice describes this processing and requires your explicit agreement. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your data in Settings and discontinuing use of the Application.

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Imported documents, extracted content, chat history, and generated insights are stored only locally on your device; delete individual documents or all data at any time in Settings
- Document content sent for AI processing is not retained by the Service Provider after processing
- Automatically collected data is retained per the third-party providers' policies
- To request deletion of any data, contact the Service Provider at asiyegy@icloud.com

## Security

The Service Provider provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the information it processes and maintains. Data in transit is encrypted.

## Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by uninstalling the Application.

## Children

The Application is not directed at children under 13, and the Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
