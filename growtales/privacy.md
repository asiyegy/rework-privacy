# Privacy Policy — GrowTales

This privacy policy applies to the GrowTales app ("Application") for mobile devices, created by Asiye ("Service Provider") as a freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

## Summary

GrowTales is a story app for families with children aged 4–7. A parent creates a story hero, and the Application generates illustrated, narrated adventure chapters inspired by a social-emotional-learning (SEL) curriculum. To create chapters and pictures, the Application sends limited, non-identifying content to a third-party AI service provider (Google). This policy explains what is collected, how, why, and with whom it is shared.

## Children's Privacy

GrowTales is designed to be used by children together with a parent or guardian, and is built to minimise data about children:

- Children never type free text into the Application; all story input comes from curated, pre-written options
- No photos, no microphone audio, and no camera access are collected — the story hero is a stylised illustrated character built from appearance options, not from a photograph
- The Application does not knowingly collect personal information from children
- There is no behavioral advertising and no third-party advertising in the Application
- Purchases, sharing, and parent settings sit behind a parental gate that children cannot pass
- Sign-in is anonymous (no name, email, or account creation is required)

## Information the Application Collects, and How

**1. Story generation data** — sent only when a new chapter, hero, or illustration is generated:

- The curated story options selected in the Application (for example, which choice the hero makes)
- The hero's appearance choices (for example, hair colour or outfit, chosen from preset options)
- The hero's illustrated reference image (an AI-generated drawing, not a photograph), reused so the hero looks the same in every chapter

**2. Automatically collected data:**

- Device information (model, operating system version, language) and IP address
- Usage data (screens visited, features used, session duration, time and date)
- Crash and diagnostic data

**3. Purchase data:** subscription status and transaction identifiers. Card details never reach the Service Provider.

## How the Information Is Used

- To generate story chapters and illustrations: selected story options, hero appearance choices, and the hero reference image are processed by AI services solely for this purpose
- To operate, maintain, and improve the Application (analytics, crash diagnostics)
- To manage subscriptions and the free-trial entitlement
- The Service Provider may use contact information you provide to send important notices

Story narration (read-aloud) is produced entirely on your device by the operating system's speech synthesiser; no audio is recorded or sent anywhere.

## Third Parties That Process Data

The Application shares data with the following processors, strictly to provide the service. Each provider is bound by data-protection terms that provide protection equal to or greater than this policy:

- [Google LLC](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) — Gemini AI models via Firebase AI Logic / Google Cloud process the selected story options, hero appearance choices, and the hero reference image to generate chapters and illustrations. Google also provides Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config, Cloud Messaging, and anonymous authentication for app operation.
- [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) — subscription management
- Apple — payment processing through the App Store

The Service Provider may also disclose information as required by law (for example, to comply with a subpoena), to protect rights or safety, to investigate fraud, or to respond to a government request.

## AI-Generated Content

Chapters and illustrations are created by artificial intelligence. They are imaginative fiction for entertainment and learning support only — they are not therapy, medical, psychological, or educational professional advice, and may occasionally contain mistakes. Google processes generation data solely to produce the requested story content; it is never sold and is not used for advertising.

## Consent

Before the Application can be used, an in-app notice describes this processing — including the use of AI — and requires explicit agreement from the parent or guardian. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your data in the Application and discontinuing use of the Application.

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Finished chapters, illustrations, and the hero are stored only locally on your device; delete them at any time from the Application
- Story generation data is not retained by the Service Provider after processing
- Automatically collected data is retained per the third-party providers' policies
- To request deletion of any data, contact the Service Provider at asiygy@icloud.com

## Security

The Service Provider provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the information it processes and maintains. Data in transit is encrypted.

## Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by uninstalling the Application.
